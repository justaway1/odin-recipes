My Red Beans and Rice

Description

This is the creamy red beans and rice everyone asks me to make.

Ingredients

  1. 1 Cup of milk
  2. 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
  3. ⅔ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  4. ½ cup white sugar
  5. 1 Cup of milk
  6. 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  7. ¼ cup butter
  8. ½ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  9. 5 ½ cups all-purpose flour

    10. Directions

    Step 1

    Place red beans into a large container and cover with several inches of cool water; let stand 8 hours to overnight. Drain.

    Step 2

    When yeast mixture is bubbling, add the cooled milk mixture. Whisk in the eggs. Stir in the remaining white sugar, salt and nutmeg. Beat the flour into the milk/egg mixture 1 cup at a time. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 to 10 minutes..

    Step 3

    SLightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 2 hours. When risen, punch down and divide dough in half.

    Step 4

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 2 cookie sheets or line with parchment paper.
